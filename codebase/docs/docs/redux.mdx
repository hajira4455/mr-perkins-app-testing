<p>In this page you will understand usage of Redux in our template</p>>

### Overview

---

<p>
  Our template uses <a href='https://react-redux.js.org/'>react-redux</a> to bind the react with redux. Store
  configurations are located in <code>@src/redux</code>.
</p>

<p>
  The globally used <code>actions</code> & <code>reducers</code> are in <code>@src/redux</code>.
</p>

<p>
  The Component specific <code>actions</code> & <code>reducers</code> are located in the <code>@src/view/</code> folder.
  Check out <code>@src/views/apps/email/store</code> for more information.
</p>
